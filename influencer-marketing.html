Frequently Asked Questions

How do you find the right influencers?

We use advanced research tools and manual vetting to find influencers whose audience demographics and values match your brand.

Do you guarantee results?

We guarantee authentic partnerships and campaign execution, but influencer marketing performance can vary based on content fit and market conditions.

How long do campaigns take?

Most campaigns run 4-8 weeks from planning to execution and reporting, depending on the scope and number of influencers.

Can you work with international influencers?

Yes, we can source both Indian and international influencers based on your target market and campaign goals.

What if an influencer doesn't deliver?

We have backup influencers ready and can replace underperforming partnerships to ensure campaign success.
